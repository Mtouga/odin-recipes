Omelet

Description

An omelet or omelette is an egg dish characterized by an elongated oval shape with a smooth surface. The inside typically has a juicy soft consistency and the outside should not be browned

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. For omelet, put the eggs and some milk in a bowl, season with salt and pepper and whisk well.
  2. Add chopped onions and parsley to the minced meat and fry briefly.
  3. Then melt the butter in a pan and ladle the egg mixture into the pan - swirling the pan to distribute the egg mixture nicely in the pan - and let it set (do not turn it).
  4. Finally, fold one half in toward the center with a spatula.
