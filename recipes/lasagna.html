Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, plural lasagnas, is the name given to both particularly wide ribbon noodles and the as well as the casserole containing several layers of dough.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 500g of minced meat
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can of peeled tomatoes
  • 300g of lasagna pasta
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot. Brown the minced meat in it all around.
  2. Add chopped onions and parsley to the minced meat and fry briefly.
  3. Finely slice the garlic and add to the minced meat. Add tomato paste as well and fry briefly.
  4. Pour the canned tomatoes, add salt and pepper. Add red wine to taste.
  5. Let the ragú boil down for at least half an hour with the pot open.
  6. Spread some Ragú Bolognese in a buttered ovenproof dish and place a layer of lasagna sheets on top.
  7. Cover the pasta layer again with ragú and then with a layer of bechamel.
  8. Then again a layer of pasta, ragú and bechamel. Thus, layer by layer fill the mold.
  9. The last layer should be the bechamel sauce. Sprinkle thickly with grated cheese and put butter flakes on top.
  10. Bake the lasagna in a hot oven at 180 °C convection oven for approx. 30 - 40 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
